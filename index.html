About

Eric Ng is a full stack developer who loves to try and understand new technologies. He obtained a Bachelor's in Economics from San Francisco State University. Worked as an accountant for California's FasTrak project. Received a certificate of completion from UC Berkeley Extension Coding Boot Camp. Became a Teaching Assistant for Trilogy Education's Data Visualization Boot Camp. Eric is currently a front end web developer for Gap Inc.

Eric also loves exploring various technologies as hobbies. Fiddling with the basics of Arduino, electronic soldering, and even 3D printing. He believes it's important to understand each technology to keep up with the rapid pace of development.
